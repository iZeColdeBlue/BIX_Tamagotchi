About the Project

BIXI is a digital pet that you can interact with on the Kunsthaus Graz. It is a playful way to engage with the building and its surroundings. The project aims to create a connection between the digital and physical worlds, allowing visitors to experience the Kunsthaus in a new and exciting way.

The project was developed as part of a collaboration between the Kunsthaus Graz and local artists. It combines elements of gaming, art, and technology to create an interactive experience that is both fun and educational.
